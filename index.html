Dubbed Anime VS Subbed Anime
Why dubbed anime is better than subbed.
  • It's better because you can watch it while doing other stuff such as cooking, or cleaning
  • It's better because you can enjoy the entire experience instead of reading the subtitles; your brain is 100% focused on the anime and not 50% on reading and 50% on watching
  • English Voice Actors are easier to follow along with what's happening and easier to understand
  • Better for binge watching
  • Sometimes subtitles don't fully translate correctly or translate text on screen, but with English dub, it translates both voice and text
  • Better if you're a slow reader
    • Why subbed anime is better than dubbed.
  • Original voice acting for original experience and culture
  • Content won't be cut for dubbing purposes (Censoring, cutting scenes)
  • Good if you're learning the language and need to know how it is spoken (slang and formal)
  • Japanese voice actors are more emotional and feel more in-character
  • More accessible and more available varied content
  • Better for recent release and on release watching, can take a while for the English dub to release
